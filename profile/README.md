# Codagotchi

**The tamagotchi for people who max out their AI tokens.**

Codagotchi is a desk-side AMOLED display that turns your Claude Code and Codex usage into a living creature. The more you code, the more your pet thrives. Let your tokens go to waste and it just sits there, waiting.

---

### What it does

**Watches your usage** — live session and weekly utilization rings so you always know how much of your budget you've burned and how long until it resets. No more hitting a wall mid-task.

**Reacts when your AI is working** — the pet wakes up and animates the moment Claude Code or Codex starts generating. Goes calm when it stops. A peripheral glance tells you if your CLI is still running.

**Feed your pet** — press the button to send a feed prompt to your CLI and interact with your AI companion. The display shows your session usage so you know exactly how deep you're in.

**Tracks your reset window** — countdown to when your token window resets, so you can time your heaviest tasks for a fresh window and stay in flow longer.

---

### Built for people who use AI seriously

If you're running Claude Code or Codex all day, you're already thinking about token budgets. Codagotchi makes that invisible context visible — on a little screen on your desk, not buried in a browser tab.

No cloud, no account, no subscription. Reads your existing CLI credentials locally and talks to the device over Bluetooth.
